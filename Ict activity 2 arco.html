The colors of the RAINBOW

RED

Red is the color that has the longest wavelength at end of the visible spectrum of light, next to orange and opposite violet. It is a primary color in the RGB color model.It's known as the color of passionate love, seduction, violence, danger, anger, and adventure.


ORANGE

O range is the colour between yellow and red on the spectrum of visible light.In traditional colour theory, it is a secondary colour of pigments, produced by mixing yellow and red. It symbolizes Promising possibility, understanding, and familiarity.


YELLOW

Y ellow is the color between green and orange on the spectrum of light.It is a primary color in subtractive color systems, used in painting or color printing.Yellow is often considered the brightest and most energizing of the warm colors. It's associated with happiness and sunshine.


GREEN

G reen is the color between cyan and yellow on the visible spectrum.It can represent new beginnings growth,renewal and abundance. Alternatively, green can also represent envy or jealousy, and greed.


BLUE

B lue is one of the three primary colours in the RYB colour model (traditional colour theory), as well as in the RGB (additive) colour model. It lies between violet and cyan on the spectrum of visible light.The color blue symbolizes trust, loyalty, calmness, and serenity.


INDIGO

I ndigo is a rich color between blue and violet on the visible spectrum, it's a dark purplish blue.It represents tranquility, harmony, confidence, and integrity.It's often associated with mystical powers, intuition and higher levels of perception.


VIOLET

V iolet is the color of light at the short wavelength end of the visible spectrum, between blue and invisible ultraviolet.n the RGB color model, violet is produced by mixing red and blue. It represents meanings of wealth, extravagance, creativity, wisdom, dignity, pride, mystery, and independence.
